Chicken and Rice Soup

Description

Whatever the mood, a bowl of spicy tasty chicken and rice soup has always breath in me,
a unique sense of such indescribable satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons (28ml) of olive oil
  • 1 pound (453.5g) of skinless, boneless chicken thighs, diced
  • 1 medium onion, diced (approximately 1 cup)
  • 1 stalk of celery, sliced into ½-inch pieces (around ¾ cup)
  • 1-2 teaspoons (2-4g) of bouillon powder
  • 2 teaspoons (4g) of [missing ingredient]
  • 1 teaspoon (2g) of [missing ingredient]
  • 2 teaspoons (2g) of fresh thyme
  • 1 tablespoon (8g) of minced garlic
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup (185g) of uncooked long grain white rice
  • 2 medium carrots, diced (approximately 1½ cups)
  • 7-8 cups (1.5-1.9l) of chicken stock (or water), adjusted to achieve the preferred soup thickness
  • Parsley, for garnish
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Begin by placing the chicken pieces in a large bowl, seasoning them with salt and black pepper,
    and setting them aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and brown them for several minutes
    until they achieve a golden color.
  3. Subsequently, incorporate the chopped onion, garlic, celery, thyme, bouillon powder, and bay leaf
    into the pot. Cook and stir the mixture for approximately six minutes, allowing the vegetables to
    soften without browning.
  4. Next, add the chicken broth or water and bring the mixture to a boil. Introduce the rice and
    carrots, seasoning with additional salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Reduce the heat to medium-low and let it simmer until the rice is tender, which should take about 20
    minutes.
  6. Finally, garnish with parsley and serve while warm.
